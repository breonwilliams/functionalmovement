Pro 360

Our vision has always been to create a system that delivers total client management capabilities. This system -- designed to be intuitive from start to finish -- provides a proven, data-driven template for medical, fitness and performance professionals. That template begins with screening and assessing movement, designing a customized program and providing comprehensive reporting capabilities to stay dialed in with client and patient progress. PRO 360 is your total client management solution, your template and was built to complement the FMS System.

Responsive Display

PRO 360 works with and for you, 24/7/365, both on and off the training floor and responds to your choice of technology.

Client Management

With Pro360, the client is always the main focus. The home page displays your client list, and with a click of a button, you can view your clientsâ€™ real-time movement profiles.

Screening

Screening has never been so easy. Using the Proâ€™s responsive display, you can use any device to complete an FMS Screen. Pro360 also empowers you to perform group screening on multiple devices.

Assessments

With the power of Pro360, medical professionals can now perform the assessment and store SFMA data. Complete with instructional videos, breakouts, and diagnosis, Pro360 allows clinicians to optimize time spent with patients for more efficient consultations.

Program Building

No matter your experience level, new ideas and training options are always a good idea. Pro360 makes it easy for you to create programs -- from basic to advanced -- using our Training Program builder and drag and drop Basic Builder.

FMS Auto-Generated Workouts

Not yet attended an FMS Level 2 course? No worries! Let Pro360 give you the power to auto-generate a corrective program based on your client's screen results. Simply complete a screen, view the report and with one simple click, generate up to an 8-week progressive program.

Exercise Library

Access over 4,000 exercises anytime, anywhere. Pro360 includes the FMS, rehab, performance and cardio library. And if you have your own awesome exercises, feel free to create your own custom exercise library -- PRO 360 will store all that for you!

Reporting

Tapping into the power of data with Pro360 as you create dynamic Reports & Visualizations of all data collected. Simply add your widgets and select your variables, pro360 does the rest for you.
